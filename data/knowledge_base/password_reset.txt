PASSWORD RESET RUNBOOK
======================

ISSUE: User forgot corporate Active Directory (AD) password
STEPS:
1. Navigate to the Self-Service Password Reset (SSPR) portal: https://sspr.corp.com. This is available from any device including personal phones.
2. Enter your corporate email address (firstname.lastname@corp.com) and click "Verify Identity".
3. Complete MFA verification using your registered authenticator app (Microsoft Authenticator or Duo). If the app is unavailable, choose the "SMS to registered mobile" option.
4. Set a new password that meets the complexity policy: minimum 12 characters, at least 1 uppercase, 1 lowercase, 1 digit, 1 special character. Password cannot contain your name or last 10 previous passwords.
5. After reset, wait up to 5 minutes for AD replication before attempting to log in to domain-joined machines or VPN.
6. If the new password is not accepted on a domain-joined laptop, lock the machine (Win+L) and unlock with the new password. Do not attempt to log in via a new session until replication completes.

ISSUE: Account is locked out (too many failed attempts)
STEPS:
1. Accounts lock after 5 consecutive failed login attempts. The lockout duration is 30 minutes and auto-unlocks after that period.
2. To unlock immediately, contact the IT helpdesk via chat at https://helpdesk.corp.com or call +1-800-555-HELP and provide your employee ID for verification.
3. Alternatively, your direct manager can submit an urgent unlock request via ServiceNow (category: Identity & Access, subcategory: Account Unlock) with your employee ID.
4. After unlock, immediately reset the password to prevent re-lock from cached wrong credentials on devices.

ISSUE: MFA device lost or replaced
STEPS:
1. Contact the Identity team at identity-support@corp.com or call the helpdesk. Identity verification will be done via employee ID + manager approval before deregistering the old device.
2. Once the old device is deregistered, enrol your new device at https://mfa.corp.com using your temporary access pass (TAP) provided by the Identity team.
3. TAPs are valid for 24 hours. If the TAP expires before enrolment, request a new one from the helpdesk.

ISSUE: Password reset required at next login (forced reset)
STEPS:
1. At the Windows login screen or web login page, you will be prompted to set a new password immediately. Follow the complexity rules listed above.
2. If the forced reset loop continues (password set but immediately prompted again), the AD attribute "User must change password at next logon" may be stuck. Contact Identity team for a manual attribute clear.

ESCALATION: Accounts not resolved within 2 hours should be escalated to the Identity & Access Management team lead via the #iam-escalations Slack channel.
